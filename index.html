About Me

My name is Julio Pochet. I'm 25 years old, born and raised in The Dominican Republic, where I graduated from High School back in 2010. Currently, I'm a Thaddeus Stevens College of Technology student pursuying a Computer Software Engineering Degree.

Since I was little, my interests have been Technology, Business, and Justice. I finished highschool at 15, and then I took 2 years off school before coming to the U.S. at 17. When I first came here I didn't know English, so I went to an English as a Second Language program over at HACC. After that I went to YTI (York Technical Institute) and obtained an Associate's in Criminal Justice & First Response.

I wanted to become a Police Officer and follow my father's steps (He's a retired Police Captain in the Dominican Republic). However, while I was in college I started a couple of online businesses and started to make decent amount of money all from my cellphone. That's where the "click" came in and I decided that I wanted to learn more about Web & Software Development so that I can build more advance ideas from scratch.

I'm very excited to learn the most that I can at Thaddeus Stevens and take advantage of all my knowledge to achieve the success I came to this country looking for.

Trust me, if you have me as a partner, you'll have someone very ambitious when it comes to pursuying his dreams.
